Business Allies
A business ally is a company that pledges support to our movement to keep Columbus Crew SC in Columbus.
  • In-Kind Support
  • Project 2019 Ticket Pledge
  • Financial: Up to $250
  • Financial: $250 to $1,000
  • Financial: Over $1,000

A business ally is a company that pledges support to our movement to keep Columbus Crew SC in Columbus, recognizing that the team adds a lot of value to the city. Beyond pledging their support, allies have contributed in a variety of other ways. We’ve created a series of symbols that recognize the different steps some of our allies have taken to Save The Crew.

  1. Financial Support – The $ symbol recognizes business allies that have contributed funds to the Save The Crew (STC) movement. The color and size of the $ symbol represents the amount of funds contributed, according to the guide below:
    • Up to $250
    • $250 to $1,000
    • Over $1,000
  2. In-Kind Support – The Gift symbol recognizes business allies that have contributed in-kind donations to the movement. These includes items such as event space, marketing assistance, free materials and services, etc.
  3. Project 2019 Ticket Pledge – The Ticket symbol recognizes business allies that have pledged to purchase season tickets or a multiple match plan for the 2019 Columbus Crew SC season, in the case that Columbus Crew SC is sold to a local investor/operator.
  4. Community Kit Sponsors – Both Compton Construction and Safelite AutoGlass get special recognition with the Jersey symbol as sponsors of The Community Kit.
